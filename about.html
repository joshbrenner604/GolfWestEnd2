Our Technology

Uneekor Launch Monitor

The Uneekor launch monitor is a professional-grade system that provides incredibly accurate ball and club data. Using advanced camera technology, it tracks:

  • Ball speed and launch angle
  • Spin rate and axis
  • Club head speed and path
  • Impact location and angle
  • Carry distance and total distance

This technology ensures that every shot you take is accurately measured and reflected in the simulation, providing a realistic and educational experience.

GSPro Software

GSPro is the premier golf simulation software that brings over 200 world-famous courses to life. Features include:

  • Stunning 4K graphics and realistic course rendering
  • Accurate physics engine for true-to-life ball flight
  • Multiple game modes including stroke play, match play, and practice
  • Detailed statistics and shot analysis
  • Regular course updates and new additions

Play courses like Pebble Beach, St. Andrews, Augusta National, and many more from the comfort of our facility.
